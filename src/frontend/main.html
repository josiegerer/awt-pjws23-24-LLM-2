Hello Learner!

We are thrilled to introduce you to LangChatAI, a revolutionary language learning companion that redefines the way you master new languages. Picture this: you and a fellow learner engage in conversations, aided by a cutting-edge Generative Agent that not only facilitates dialogue but actively corrects your mistakes in real time. No more dull interactions with robotic bots—LangChatAI offers dynamic, interactive, and, above all, fun learning experiences. This innovative tool adapts seamlessly to your level of understanding, ensuring each conversation is tailored to your progress.

Key Features:

Real-time Mistakes Detection and Correction:

LangChatAI’s advanced Generative Agent detects and corrects mistakes on the fly, providing explanations to help you grasp the nuances of your errors. Plus, you can review and summarize your chat sessions after the conversation.
Mistakes Analysis and Learning Suggestions:

Analyzes frequently made mistakes and suggests relevant learning units to the learner to correct these mistakes and deepen their understanding as a summary.
Companion Simulation:

User starts a conversation on a chosen topic and language with the Agent. After ending the conversation Agent provides Feedback to the user.
